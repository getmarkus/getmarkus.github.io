I created a mashup of the [Jekyll](http://www.getmdl.io/components/index.html#layout-section) static site generator and Google's [Material Design Lite](http://www.getmdl.io/) style guide.  

[https://github.com/getmarkus/jekyll-material-design-lite](https://github.com/getmarkus/jekyll-material-design-lite)<!--more-->

Primarily, I focused on the [Layouts](http://www.getmdl.io/components/index.html#layout-section) section of MDL.  

It still needs some work to be able to make fully polished MDL websites, but it is a good starting point.  It worked out well, mostly because of MDL's flexible architecture.
